Applied Artificial Intelligence

Back to previous page

Summary

Based on a research by Microsoft about the AI adoption in Southeast Asia, Indonesia was one of the countries which still lack of the adoption of AI. There was only 14% of companies in Indonesia which really applied and adopted the technology of AI within their systems. The lack of applied AI in Indonesia is because the low awareness and the lack of skill required by the human capital related to the development of AI technology.
Image : nodeflux

Machine Learning and Predictive Analysis

Although the applied AI is still limited in Indonesia, there is a hopeful future in the development of AI knowledge as there are many communities, courses, and bootcamps which provide Indonesians with the knowledge of AI, machine learning, and the predictive analysis. As the development of AI is closely related to technology, many Indonesian millennials and Gen Z start to learn the skill of mastering machine learning through some classes and workshops. The development of machine learning and predictive analysis in Indonesia is also influencing the growth of startups which core business is related to applied AI.

Industrial Artificial Intelligence

In Indonesia, industrial AI is commonly used by e-commerce or apps companies. The application of AI in idustry is also related with the required skill that needs to be owned by human resources in Indonesia. Not to mention that the applied AI in industrial sector will also create another issue which related to unemployment. Although the development is still growing, AI in Indonesia will surely create a disruption in the job landscape and its human capital.
